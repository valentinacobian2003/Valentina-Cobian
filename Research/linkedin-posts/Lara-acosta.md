LinkedIn gurus will tell you to focus on the hook.

But all my best-performing posts have more than one.

Here are 4 writing tips I use daily to write viral content:

1. Write each sentence like its the last: Be precise.
2. Add a "quick win" in each line: Make it applicable.
3. Stop over-explaining: Get to the point, immediately.
4. Give solutions: Give people a reason to comeback!

Three bonus writing tips:

Use bold statements, people like confidence.
Be immediately actionable, your post is their guide.
Avoid complicated words, write conversationally.

This is how you get people hooked on your content.
And also, how you get them to stay.

Write a master hook. In every. single. line.
