## Post 1
Date: May 2025 (approx)
URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alicjasmin_my-best-advice-for-beginners-on-linkedin-activity-7317494188671963137-NLA5?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_ios&rcm=ACoAADVDl30BFFnak4fNuobO-66pqRkE3P_9C9g

My best advice for beginners on LinkedIn:

Start fast → Adapt slowly → Decide firmly.

(First, Save this and Repost ♻️)

Now, let's talk about what every beginner feels:

"I have no idea what/when to post"
"Oh, cool hack - I need to try this out"
"I'll start tomorrow, it's overwhelming now"

Now, let's simplify:

 1. Start by commenting daily. On 10-20 posts daily.
 2. Then, by writing 1 post weekly. Then, 2 weekly.
 3. Then, start sending DMs + connect requests.
 4. Then, increase to 50 comments and 5 posts.
 5. Then, start looking at your Profile Viewers.
 6. Then, DM 5-10 Profile Viewers - daily too.
 7. Then, launch a proper offer. Confidently!
 8. Then, keep a slower content frequency 
 9. Focus 90% of time onto DM outreach
10. Comment daily to 10x your visibility

And then simply...

Repeat steps 8-10 until you're tired of it. Then, adapt again by doing 1-7 to bring back some motivation.

---

This is what I mean by "Adapt slowly".

You think a launch happens just like that?
And everyone mega-comments out the gate?
And you have the confidence to DM prospects? 

No. This takes time.

I'm just trying to help you not be overwhelmed on the journey. Trust me, I've been there. 

And I quit. Two years in a row.

I don't want that to be you. 😊

Monday Motivation, over. Share it with a friend! ♻️

## Post 2
Date: May 2025 (approx)
URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alicjasmin_good-news-i-made-a-7-day-linkedin-plan-for-activity-7320393287423475712-nqR0?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_ios&rcm=ACoAADVDl30BFFnak4fNuobO-66pqRkE3P_9C9g

Good news! I made a 7-day LinkedIn plan for you.

Actually... 3 plans for 3 stages → find yours.

(Save this post + Repost before continuing)

Beginner: Needs consistency (first 1-2 months)

Day 1: Spend the day optimizing your profile (all of it)
Day 2: Unfollow everyone "irrelevant" in your feed 
Day 3: Comment on 20+ posts in your new feed
Day 4: Write your personal introduction post
Day 5: Write your first "advice" type post
Day 6: Send 5 DMs to 5 commenters
Day 7: Repeat 3, 5 and 6 weekly

Intermediate: Needs growth (2-12 months)

Day 1: Write your next 7 posts for the entire week
Day 2: Publish the 1st post → 3-4 per week is ok
Day 3: Start commenting on 20+ creators daily
Day 4: Create an in-depth post (3k characters)
Day 5: Create a shorter post > 300 characters
Day 6: Understand top formats → repurpose 
Day 7: Collaborate with an expert (1x week)

Pro: Needs to scale revenue (12+ months)

Day 1: Spend the entire day crafting an offer funnel
Day 2: Publish a post that shifts limiting beliefs 
Day 3: Publish a post describing your offer
Day 4: Publish a process breakdown post
Day 5: Publish a "hard sell" type of post
Day 6: Publish a "personal story"post
Day 7: Spend the entire day DM-ing

Whatever stage you're at, remember:

1. Content drives visibility and shifts mindsets
2. Comments can deepen any relationship
3. DMs turn relationships into deals
   
---

Who knows, maybe you can even mix-and-match these 3 approaches for your situation.

There is no one-size-fits-all LinkedIn strategy.

(This is just what I'd recommend at each stage)

But there are proven steps you can't miss! All here.

Hopefully, this helps. (Share it further ♻️)

## Post 3
Date: October 2025 (approx)
URL: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alicjasmin_i-received-214954-comments-this-year-but-activity-7368953235195297796-zAWw?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_ios&rcm=ACoAADVDl30BFFnak4fNuobO-66pqRkE3P_9C9g

I received 214,954 comments this year.

But I only posted 125 times. How?

Here's something I aim to do with every post, no matter the topic, no matter the day of posting:

(I'll include the "why" with each one)

1. Stay for 1 hour on each post → This builds my community + ensures people keep showing up. When your audience feels seen and heard, they stay.

Why: Engagement doesn't fluctuate post-to-post.

2. Alternate between Most Recent and Most Relevant comments → I make sure I speak to "new" people daily. This amplifies no. 1 above and brings new reach.

Why: Algorithm doesn't think you're a robot.

3. Add extra ways to engage → I add 5-7 "pinned" comments under each post. I ask questions. I share behind-the-scenes. I share bonus resources.

Why 1: People also talk to each other, not just me.

Why 2: I find new leads + Link Up signups this way.

4. Ask a question in the P.S. of my post

Why: Because psychology proves that people need "permission" to engage with you. Subconsciously. That little question in the end → that's your invite.

Why: More engagement + amplifies all 3 above.

---

I've been doing this for years. It works.
